About

HomeWork solutions is a platform to help students solve and understand the assignments given to them at school. On this platform, students get to upload their assignments and get solutions after a given time. There are also opportunities for students to communicate with themselves and share solutions.

HomeWork solutions is concerned more about the process than the end result. we believe in bridging the gap in the classroom, and this can only be achieved by everybody coming together and contributing.

HomeWork solutions are a team of quality people commited to seeing growth in each and everyone of our users. We have a vision to see increased success in the educational system by helping provide solutions to your classroom problems.
